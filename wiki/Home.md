## üí° Key Principles

The framework is built on five principles:

1. **Business-First** - Always connect to business value
2. **Pragmatic** - Balance thoroughness with speed
3. **User-Centric** - User experience matters most
4. **Risk-Aware** - Understand what can go wrong
5. **Iterative** - Continuous improvement, not one-time evaluation
